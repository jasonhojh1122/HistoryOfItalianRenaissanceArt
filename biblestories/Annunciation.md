# Annunciation

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annunciation)

天使報喜

## Summary

The Annunciation depicts the announcement by the Angel Gabriel to the Virgin Mary that she would conceive and bear the Son of God. Gabriel appears to Mary in Nazareth and tells her "Hail, full of grace, the Lord is with thee." He announces that she will conceive by the Holy Spirit and bear a son named Jesus who will be called the Son of the Most High and will reign over the house of Jacob forever.

## Biblical Reference

**Book(s)**: Gospel of Luke
**Chapters/Verses**: Luke 1:26-38
