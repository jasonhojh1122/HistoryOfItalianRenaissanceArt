# Creation of Adam

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Creation_of_Adam)

## Summary

The Creation of Adam depicts God creating the first man. According to Genesis, God formed Adam from the dust of the ground and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life, making him a living being. God then placed Adam in the Garden of Eden to tend and keep it. This foundational story establishes humanity's special relationship with God and the beginning of human history.

## Biblical Reference

**Book(s)**: Book of Genesis
**Chapters/Verses**: Genesis 2:7
