# Nativity

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nativity_of_Jesus)

耶穌降生

## Summary

The Nativity depicts the birth of Jesus Christ in Bethlehem. According to the Gospels, Joseph and Mary traveled to Bethlehem for the census ordered by Caesar Augustus. Finding no room at the inn, Mary gave birth to Jesus in humble circumstances and laid him in a manger. The birth was announced to shepherds by angels, and they came to worship the newborn.

## Biblical Reference

**Book(s)**: Gospel of Luke, Gospel of Matthew
**Chapters/Verses**: Luke 2:1-20, Matthew 1:18-25
