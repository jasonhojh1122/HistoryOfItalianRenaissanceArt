Artworks

St. John the Baptist

Bronze

Part of the sculptural program at Orsanmichele. Ghiberti developed a new approach to sculpting eyes, cutting out the line of the cornea and dot of the pupil.

North Door of Florence Baptistery

Bronze

The second set of bronze doors for the Florence Baptistery, created after Ghiberti won the famous competition against Brunelleschi.

Gates of Paradise

Gilded bronze

The third and most famous set of doors for the Florence Baptistery. Contains the first sensuous female nudes of the Renaissance. Named "Gates of Paradise" by Michelangelo.

Competition Panel (Ghiberti)

Bronze relief

Ghiberti's winning competition panel for the second set of bronze doors for the Florence Baptistery. Only the Isaac figure was cast separately, making the whole piece lighter but stronger.
