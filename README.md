### Must-See Works

- **Dome of Florence Cathedral** - [Florence Cathedral](locations/FlorenceCathedral.md) - Brunelleschi's engineering masterpiece
- **Gates of Paradise** - [Baptistery of Florence](locations/BaptisteryOfFlorence.md) - Ghiberti's gilded bronze doors
- **Bronze David** - [Bargello](locations/Bargello.md) - Donatello's first large-scale Renaissance nude
- **Trinity** - [Santa Maria Novella](locations/SantaMariaNovella.md) - Masaccio's revolutionary perspective fresco
- **Annunciation** - [Museo di San Marco](locations/MuseoDiSanMarco.md) - Fra Angelico's serene masterwork
- **Giotto's Frescoes** - [Santa Croce](locations/SantaCroce.md) - Life of St. Francis cycle
- **Enthroned Madonna** - [Uffizi Gallery](locations/UffiziGallery.md) - Cimabue's iconic Italo-Byzantine altarpiece
- **Tabernacle** - [Orsanmichele](locations/Orsanmichele.md) - Orcagna's ornate marble shrine
- **Battle of San Romano** - [Uffizi Gallery](locations/UffiziGallery.md) - Uccello's perspective tour de force
- **South Door** - [Baptistery of Florence](locations/BaptisteryOfFlorence.md) - Andrea Pisano's bronze panels
