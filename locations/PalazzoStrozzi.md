# Palazzo Strozzi

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palazzo_Strozzi)

[GoogleMap](https://maps.google.com/?q=Palazzo%20Strozzi%20Florence%20Italy)

Florence, Italy

**Type**: Palazzo

Palazzo Strozzi is a palace in Florence located in the historic centre between Via Strozzi, Piazza Strozzi, and Via Tornabuoni. Designed by Benedetto da Maiano.
