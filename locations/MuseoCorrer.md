# Museo Correr

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Museo_Correr)

[GoogleMap](https://maps.google.com/?q=Museo%20Correr%20Venice%20Italy)

Venice, Italy

**Type**: Museum

The Museo Correr is a museum in Venice located in St. Mark's Square. It is one of the 11 civic museums run by the Fondazione Musei Civici di Venezia. The museum extends along the southside of the square on the upper floors of the Procuratorie Nuove.
