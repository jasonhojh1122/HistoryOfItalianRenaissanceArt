# San Marco

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Marco,_Florence)

[GoogleMap](https://maps.google.com/?q=San%20Marco%20Florence%20Italy)

Florence, Italy

**Type**: Church / Convent

San Marco is a religious complex in Florence. It comprises a church and a convent which was formerly a Dominican friary. The convent, which is now a museum, has three claims to fame: it houses the Museo di San Marco and was the home of the Dominican preacher Girolamo Savonarola.
