### Competition panels for the second set of bronze doors
- **Artists**: [Filippo Brunelleschi](../artists/FilippoBrunelleschi.md) (left) and [Lorenzo Ghiberti](../artists/LorenzoGhiberti.md) (right)
- Brunelleschi: Servant's pose derived from Spinario, each figure cast individually
- Ghiberti: Only Isaac cast separately, making the piece lighter but stronger
