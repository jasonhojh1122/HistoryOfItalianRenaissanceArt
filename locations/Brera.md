# Pinacoteca di Brera

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pinacoteca_di_Brera)

[GoogleMap](https://maps.google.com/?q=Pinacoteca%20di%20Brera%20Milan%20Italy)

Milan, Italy

**Type**: Museum

The Pinacoteca di Brera is the main public gallery for paintings in Milan. It contains one of the foremost collections of Italian paintings from the 13th to the 20th century, an outgrowth of the cultural program of the Brera Academy, which shares the site in the Palazzo Brera.
