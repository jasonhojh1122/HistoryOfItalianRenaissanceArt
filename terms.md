# Art History Terms

## Iconographic Types

### Christus triumphans
A cross depicting a living Christ, showing him triumphant over death.

### Christus patiens
A cross depicting a dead Christ, emphasizing his suffering and sacrifice.

### Tondo
A circular painting or relief sculpture. The term comes from the Italian word "rotondo" (round).

## Names

### Francesco
