# Art History Terms

## Religious & Iconographic Terms

### Christus triumphans
A cross depicting a living Christ, showing him triumphant over death.

### Christus patiens
A cross depicting a dead Christ, emphasizing his suffering and sacrifice.

### Annunciation
天使報喜 - The announcement by the Angel Gabriel to the Virgin Mary that she would conceive and bear the Son of God (Luke 1:26-38).

### Nativity
耶穌降生 - The birth of Jesus Christ in Bethlehem (Luke 2:1-20, Matthew 1:18-25).

### Flight into Egypt
The journey of the Holy Family (Mary, Joseph, and the infant Jesus) to Egypt to escape King Herod's massacre of infants (Matthew 2:13-23).

### Presentation in the Temple
The ritual presentation of the infant Jesus at the Temple in Jerusalem, forty days after his birth (Luke 2:22-40).

### Francesco
方濟各 - Francis (as in St. Francis of Assisi).
