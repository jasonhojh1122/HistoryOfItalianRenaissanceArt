# Andrea Mantegna

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrea_Mantegna)

**Born**: c. 1431, Isola di Carturo, Republic of Venice
**Died**: 1506, Mantua

Italian Renaissance painter, a student of Roman archaeology, and the son-in-law of Jacopo Bellini. Brother-in-law of Giovanni and Gentile Bellini.
