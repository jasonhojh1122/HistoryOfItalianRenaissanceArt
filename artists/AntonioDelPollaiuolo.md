# Antonio del Pollaiuolo

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antonio_del_Pollaiuolo)

**Born**: c. 1429, Florence
**Died**: 1498, Rome

Italian Renaissance painter, sculptor, engraver, and goldsmith, who made important works in all these media. Known for excelling in subjects of action and dynamic movement.
