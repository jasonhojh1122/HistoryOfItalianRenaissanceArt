# Donatello

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donatello)

**Born**: c. 1386, Florence, Republic of Florence
**Died**: December 13, 1466, Florence

Born Donato di Niccol√≤ di Betto Bardi, Donatello was an Italian sculptor of the Renaissance period. He studied classical sculpture and developed an Early Renaissance style. Belonged to the Arte di Pietra e Legname, the guild of workers in stone and wood. His periods in Rome, Padua, and Siena introduced his techniques to other parts of Italy.
