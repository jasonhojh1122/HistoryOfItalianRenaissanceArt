# Giovanni di Bicci de' Medici

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giovanni_di_Bicci_de'_Medici)

**Born**: 1360, Florence
**Died**: February 20, 1429, Florence

Italian banker and founder of the Medici Bank. His founding of the family bank truly initiated the Medici family's rise to power in Florence. Father of Cosimo de' Medici and major patron of Renaissance art.
