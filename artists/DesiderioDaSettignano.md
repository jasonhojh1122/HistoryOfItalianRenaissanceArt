# Desiderio da Settignano

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desiderio_da_Settignano)

**Born**: c. 1430, Settignano, Republic of Florence
**Died**: 1464, Florence

Italian Renaissance sculptor active in north Italy. Known as a stonecutter, he was one of the finest marble sculptors of the early Renaissance.
