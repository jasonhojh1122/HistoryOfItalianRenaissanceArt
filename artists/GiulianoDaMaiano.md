# Giuliano da Maiano

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giuliano_da_Maiano)

**Born**: 1432, Maiano, Republic of Florence
**Died**: 1490, Naples

Italian architect, intarsia-worker, and sculptor, the elder brother of Benedetto da Maiano, with whom he often collaborated.
