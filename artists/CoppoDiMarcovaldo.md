# Coppo di Marcovaldo

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coppo_di_Marcovaldo)

**Born**: c. 1225, Florence, Republic of Florence
**Died**: c. 1276, Siena

Florentine painter in the Italo-Byzantine style, active in the mid-thirteenth century. His fusion of Italian and Byzantine styles had great influence on generations of Italian artists.
