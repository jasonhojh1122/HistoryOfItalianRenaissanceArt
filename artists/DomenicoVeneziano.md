# Domenico Veneziano

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domenico_Veneziano)

**Born**: c. 1410, Venice, Republic of Venice
**Died**: May 15, 1461, Florence

Italian painter of the early Renaissance, active mostly in Perugia and Tuscany. Known for his use of light and color, he was an important influence on Piero della Francesca.
