# Equestrian Statue of Bernabò Visconti

- **Artist**: Bonino da Campione
- **Location**: [Castello Sforzesco](../locations/CastelloSforzesco.md), Milan
- **Medium**: Marble
- **Date**: c. 1363

## Description

Equestrian monument of Bernabò Visconti, Lord of Milan.
