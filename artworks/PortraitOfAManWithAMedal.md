# Portrait of a Man with a Medal of Cosimo de Medici

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portrait_of_a_Man_with_a_Medal_of_Cosimo_the_Elder)

- **Artist**: [Sandro Botticelli](../artists/SandroBotticelli.md)
- **Location**: [Uffizi Gallery](../locations/UffiziGallery.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel
- **Date**: c. 1475

## Description

The man may be Lorenzo di Pierfrancesco, holding a medal depicting Cosimo de' Medici.
