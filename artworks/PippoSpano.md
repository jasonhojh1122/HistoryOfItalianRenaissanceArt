# Pippo Spano

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Famous_Men_and_Women_(Andrea_del_Castagno))

- **Artist**: [Andrea del Castagno](../artists/AndreaDelCastagno.md)
- **Location**: [Uffizi Gallery](../locations/UffiziGallery.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Fresco (detached)
- **Date**: c. 1450

## Description

Portrait of Filippo Scolari, known as Pippo Spano. Born in Florence, he became a general of King Sigismund and fought in the Hussite Wars, where he was beaten by Jan Žižka.
