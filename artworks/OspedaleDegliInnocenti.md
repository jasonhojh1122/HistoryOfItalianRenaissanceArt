# Ospedale degli Innocenti

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ospedale_degli_Innocenti)

- **Artist**: [Brunelleschi](../artists/FilippoBrunelleschi.md)
- **Location**: [Ospedale degli Innocenti](../locations/OspedaleDegliInnocenti.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Architecture

## Description

Hospital of the Innocents, designed by Brunelleschi. Provided orphans and children with housing and education until age 18. Children were given the last name "Innocenti."
