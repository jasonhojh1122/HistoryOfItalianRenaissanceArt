## Biblical Context

Depicts the moment when the apostle Thomas, who had doubted the resurrection, touches the wounds of the risen Christ. From John 20:24-29, Jesus says to Thomas, "Put your finger here; see my hands. Reach out your hand and put it into my side. Stop doubting and believe."
