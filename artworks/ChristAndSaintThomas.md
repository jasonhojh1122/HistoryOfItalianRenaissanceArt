# Christ and Saint Thomas

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christ_and_Saint_Thomas_(Verrocchio))

- **Artist**: [Andrea del Verrocchio](../artists/AndreaDelVerrocchio.md)
- **Location**: [Orsanmichele](../locations/Orsanmichele.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Bronze

## Biblical Context

Depicts the moment when the apostle Thomas, who had doubted the resurrection, touches the wounds of the risen Christ. From John 20:24-29, Jesus says to Thomas, "Put your finger here; see my hands. Reach out your hand and put it into my side. Stop doubting and believe."

## Description

Bronze sculpture group at Orsanmichele.
