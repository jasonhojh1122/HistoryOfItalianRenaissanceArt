# St. Francis Undergoing the Test by Fire

- **Artist**: [Giotto](../artists/GiottoDiBondone.md)
- **Location**: [Santa Croce](../locations/SantaCroce.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Fresco

## Description

Depicts St. Francis undergoing the test by fire before the sultan of Egypt. Contains one of the earliest known representations of black people in Western art.
