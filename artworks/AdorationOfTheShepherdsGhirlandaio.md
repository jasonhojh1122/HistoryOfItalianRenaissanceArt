# Adoration of the Shepherds

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adoration_of_the_Shepherds_(Ghirlandaio))

- **Artist**: [Domenico Ghirlandaio](../artists/DomenicoGhirlandaio.md)
- **Location**: [Basilica di Santa Trinita](../locations/BasilicaDiSantaTrinita.md), Sassetti Chapel, Florence
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel
- **Date**: 1485

## Biblical Context

[Adoration of the Shepherds](../biblestories/AdorationOfTheShepherds.md) - The visit of the shepherds to the newborn Jesus in Bethlehem, as described in Luke 2:8-20. An angel appeared to shepherds keeping watch over their flocks and announced the birth of the Savior.

## Description

Altarpiece for the Sassetti Chapel.
