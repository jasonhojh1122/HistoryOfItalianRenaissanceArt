# Equestrian Statue of Bartolomeo Colleoni

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equestrian_statue_of_Bartolomeo_Colleoni_(Venice))

- **Artist**: [Andrea del Verrocchio](../artists/AndreaDelVerrocchio.md)
- **Location**: [Basilica dei Santi Giovanni e Paolo](../locations/SantiGiovanniEPaolo.md), Venice
- **Medium**: Bronze

## Description

Monumental equestrian statue of the condottiero (mercenary leader) Bartolomeo Colleoni, located in the Campo dei Santi Giovanni e Paolo in Venice.
