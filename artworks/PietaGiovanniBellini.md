# Pietà

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Dead_Christ_Supported_by_the_Virgin_and_Saint_John)

- **Artist**: [Giovanni Bellini](../artists/GiovanniBellini.md)
- **Location**: [Pinacoteca di Brera](../locations/Brera.md), Milan
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel
- **Date**: c. 1465–1470

## Biblical Context

[Lamentation](../biblestories/Lamentation.md) - The Virgin Mary mourning over the body of Christ after the Crucifixion. While not directly described in scripture, it is derived from the burial accounts and represents the sorrow of Mary.

## Description

An intimate depiction of the dead Christ supported by the Virgin Mary.
