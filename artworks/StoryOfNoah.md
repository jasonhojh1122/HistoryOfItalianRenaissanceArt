# Story of Noah

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frescoes_in_the_Chiostro_Verde)

- **Artist**: [Paolo Uccello](../artists/PaoloUccello.md)
- **Location**: [Santa Maria Novella](../locations/SantaMariaNovella.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Fresco
- **Date**: c. 1447

## Biblical Context

[Story of Noah](../biblestories/StoryOfNoah.md) - Scenes from Genesis 6-9, including the great flood sent by God to cleanse the earth of wickedness, Noah's construction of the ark, and the eventual receding of the waters.

## Description

Fresco depicting scenes from the Story of Noah in the Chiostro Verde (Green Cloister), demonstrating Uccello's fascination with perspective.
