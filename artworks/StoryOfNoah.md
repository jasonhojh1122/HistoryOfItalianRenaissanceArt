# Story of Noah

- **Artist**: [Paolo Uccello](../artists/PaoloUccello.md)
- **Location**: [Santa Maria Novella](../locations/SantaMariaNovella.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Fresco

## Description

Fresco depicting scenes from the Story of Noah, demonstrating Uccello's fascination with perspective.
