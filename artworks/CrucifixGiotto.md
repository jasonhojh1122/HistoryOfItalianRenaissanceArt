# Crucifix

- **Artist**: [Giotto](../artists/GiottoDiBondone.md)
- **Location**: [Santa Maria Novella](../locations/SantaMariaNovella.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel

## Description

The earliest known artwork by Giotto, dating to approximately 1290-1300.
