# Last Judgement

- **Artist**: [Pietro Cavallini](../artists/PietroCavallini.md)
- **Location**: [Santa Cecilia in Trastevere](../locations/SantaCeciliaInTrastevere.md), Rome
- **Medium**: Fresco

## Description

Notable for Cavallini's innovative use of light source in his painting, a significant advancement in the Duecento period.
