# Enthroned Madonna and Child with Angels and Prophets

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Santa_Trinita_Maestà)

- **Artist**: [Cimabue](../artists/GiovanniCimabue.md)
- **Location**: [Uffizi Gallery](../locations/UffiziGallery.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel
- **Date**: c. 1280–1290

## Biblical Context

[Madonna and Child](../biblestories/MadonnaAndChild.md) - The Virgin Mary with the infant Jesus, representing the Incarnation of God.

## Description

Also known as the Santa Trinita Maestà. Cimabue was the last great Italo-Byzantine painter.
