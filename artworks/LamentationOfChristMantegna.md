# The Foreshortened Christ (Lamentation of Christ)

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lamentation_of_Christ_(Mantegna))

- **Artist**: [Andrea Mantegna](../artists/AndreaMantegna.md)
- **Location**: [Pinacoteca di Brera](../locations/Brera.md), Milan
- **Medium**: Tempera on canvas
- **Date**: c. 1480

## Biblical Context

The Lamentation depicts the mourning over Christ's body after the Crucifixion and before his burial. While not directly described in scripture, it is derived from the burial accounts in the Gospels (Matthew 27:57-61, Mark 15:42-47, Luke 23:50-56, John 19:38-42).

## Description

Famous for its dramatic use of foreshortening, showing Christ's body from the feet with the wounds in his feet prominently visible.
