## Biblical Context

David, the young shepherd who slew the giant Goliath with a stone from his sling, as told in 1 Samuel 17. This victory made him a hero of Israel and he later became king.

## Description

Bronze sculpture depicting David as a young man, with leather cloth and a smiling expression instead of the uncertain expression seen in earlier depictions.
