# Procession of the True Cross in Piazza San Marco

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Procession_in_St._Mark's_Square)

- **Artist**: [Gentile Bellini](../artists/GentileBellini.md)
- **Location**: [Gallerie dell'Accademia](../locations/Accademia.md), Venice
- **Medium**: Tempera on canvas
- **Date**: c. 1496

## Description

Painted for the scuole (school) of Venice, a religious group. Shows a procession in Piazza San Marco with remarkable architectural detail.
