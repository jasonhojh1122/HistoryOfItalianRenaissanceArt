## Biblical Context

[Last Supper](../biblestories/LastSupper.md) - Jesus's final meal with his twelve disciples before his crucifixion, during which he instituted the Eucharist (Matthew 26:17-30).

## Description

Frescoes in the Refectory of Santa Croce. Notable for placing Judas on "our" side of the table, a convention that continued until Leonardo's Last Supper.
