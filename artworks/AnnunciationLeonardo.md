## Biblical Context

[Annunciation](../biblestories/Annunciation.md) - The announcement by the angel Gabriel to the Virgin Mary that she would conceive and bear a son through the Holy Spirit (Luke 1:26-38).

## Description

An early work by Leonardo showing the angel Gabriel appearing to the Virgin Mary. The painting demonstrates Leonardo's early mastery of perspective and atmospheric effects.
