# San Giobbe Altarpiece

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Giobbe_Altarpiece)

- **Artist**: [Giovanni Bellini](../artists/GiovanniBellini.md)
- **Location**: [Gallerie dell'Accademia](../locations/Accademia.md), Venice
- **Medium**: Oil on panel
- **Date**: c. 1487

## Description

A sacra conversazione (sacred conversation) altarpiece showing the Madonna and Child with saints.
