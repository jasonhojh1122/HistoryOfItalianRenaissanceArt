- **Artist**: [Donatello](../artists/Donatello.md)
- **Location**: [Bargello](../locations/Bargello.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Marble
- **Date**: 1416-1417

## Description

Originally part of the sculptural program at Orsanmichele, now in the Bargello.
