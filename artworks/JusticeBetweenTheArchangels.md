# Justice Between the Archangels Michael and Gabriel

- **Artist**: [Jacobello del Fiore](../artists/JacobelloDelFiore.md)
- **Location**: [Gallerie dell'Accademia](../locations/Accademia.md), Venice
- **Medium**: Tempera on panel

## Description

A triptych depicting the personification of Justice flanked by the Archangels Michael and Gabriel.
