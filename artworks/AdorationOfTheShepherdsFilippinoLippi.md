# Adoration of the Shepherds

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adoration_of_the_Shepherds_(Filippino_Lippi))

- **Artist**: [Filippino Lippi](../artists/FilippinoLippi.md)
- **Location**: [Uffizi Gallery](../locations/UffiziGallery.md), Florence
- **Medium**: Oil on panel
- **Date**: c. 1480

## Biblical Context

[Adoration of the Shepherds](../biblestories/AdorationOfTheShepherds.md) - The visit of the shepherds to the newborn Jesus in Bethlehem, as described in Luke 2:8-20. An angel appeared to shepherds keeping watch over their flocks and announced the birth of the Savior.

## Description

Painting by Filippino Lippi, son of Filippo Lippi.
